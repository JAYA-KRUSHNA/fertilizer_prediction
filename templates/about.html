Our Mission

CropWise AI is dedicated to transforming agricultural practices through intelligent fertilizer management. We combine cutting-edge machine learning technology with agricultural science to help farmers optimize fertilizer usage, reduce environmental impact, and improve crop productivity.

Our platform analyzes soil composition, crop requirements, and climate patterns to deliver precise, data-driven recommendations that save resources and boost yields.

Technology & Methodology

Data-Driven Approach

Our system uses comprehensive agricultural datasets including soil properties, climate variables, and crop characteristics to train accurate prediction models.

Machine Learning Models

Dataset Overview

Comprehensive Agricultural Dataset

Our dataset includes 10,000+ synthetic agricultural samples covering various crops, soil types, and climatic conditions. Each sample contains:

  • Soil Properties: pH, texture, nutrient levels (NPK), organic matter content
  • Crop Information: Type (wheat, rice, maize, soybean, cotton)
  • Climate Variables: Temperature, humidity, rainfall patterns
  • Irrigation Methods: Rainfed, drip, sprinkler, flood irrigation
  • Fertilizer Requirements: Calculated optimal fertilizer amounts
